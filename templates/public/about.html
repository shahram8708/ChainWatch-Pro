We're on a mission to eliminate supply chain blind spots.

ChainWatch Pro helps operations teams detect disruption early, decide faster with confidence, and protect customer commitments at scale. We believe visibility and execution should work as one system, not separate tools.

Why We Built ChainWatch Pro

In 2026, one of our founding teams watched a critical shipment portfolio stall behind a sudden congestion wave and learned about it only after customer escalations began. The resulting service failures and emergency freight decisions cost over ₹2.3 crore in less than six weeks, and every discussion felt reactive because no one had clear early-warning visibility.

That experience highlighted a structural problem: logistics teams had data, but not decision-ready intelligence. Carrier portals were fragmented, status feeds were inconsistent, and risk signals were discovered too late. The platform we envisioned had to combine continuous disruption detection with actionable alternatives, not just another dashboard.

ChainWatch Pro was built to close that gap. We design for operators who need speed with governance, and for leaders who need confidence that every intervention is measurable, auditable, and tied to business outcomes.

Our Core Philosophy

Visibility Before Reaction

Every shipment must be continuously observable. "No news is not good news" and the absence of data is itself a risk signal that requires investigation.

Intelligence Before Action

Decisions must be grounded in scored risk data, not intuition. We quantify uncertainty and provide confidence-ranked recommendations teams can explain and trust.

Speed Before Precision

A 90% accurate rerouting recommendation in 5 minutes is more valuable than a 99% accurate one in 3 hours. We optimize for decision velocity where it matters most.
